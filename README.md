# Loan Analysis Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **Loan Power BI Project** provides a comprehensive analysis of loan distribution, default patterns, applicant demographics, and financial risk metrics.
This dashboard aims to help financial institutions understand borrower behavior, assess risk levels, and make data-driven lending decisions.

---

## Dashboard Sections

### **1. Loan Default & Overview**
This page provides a high-level view of loan trends and default patterns.

#### **Key Insights:**
- **Loan Amount by Purpose:**
  - Highest loan amounts are taken for **Home** and **Business** purposes.
- **Average Income by Employment:**
  - **Full-time employees** have the highest average income, while **unemployed** individuals have the lowest.
- **Default Rate by Employment Type:**
  - The **unemployed** category shows the highest default rate.
- **Average Loan Amount by Age Group:**
  - **Adults** have the highest average loan amounts, while **teens** have the lowest.
- **Default Rate by Year:**
  - The highest default rates occurred in **2015 and 2016**.

---

### **2. Applicant Demographic & Financial Profile**
This page focuses on applicant characteristics such as age, marital status, credit score, and education level.

#### **Key Insights:**
- **Medium Loan Amount by Credit Score:**
  - Borrowers with **medium credit scores** have the highest average loan amount.
- **Average Loan Amount (High Credit) by Age & Marital Status:**
  - **Married adults** tend to have slightly higher loan amounts across all age groups.
- **Total Loan (Adults) by Credit Score Bins:**
  - Most loans are concentrated in the **medium credit score** category.
- **Loan (Middle Age) by Mortgage/Dependent:**
  - Having a mortgage or dependents does not significantly affect the total loan amount.
- **Number of Loans by Education Type:**
  - **Bachelor's degree holders** have the highest number of loans, followed by **high school graduates**.

---

### **3. Financial Risk Metrics**
This section focuses on year-over-year (YOY) trends and risk evaluation.

#### **Key Insights:**
- **YOY Loan Amount Change:**
  - Loan amounts fluctuated annually, with significant growth in **2015** and **2018**.
- **YOY Default Loan Change:**
  - Default rates peaked in **2015** and dropped significantly in **2017** before rising again in **2018**.
- **YTD Loan Amount by Credit Score & Marital Status:**
  - **Medium credit score** and **married applicants** hold the largest portion of total loans.
- **Loan Distribution by Income Bracket & Employment Type:**
  - **High-income, full-time employees** contribute the largest share of the total loan amount.
